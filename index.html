Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you an investor or a builder?
Both. We are a venture builder with capital and a hands-on product/engineering core. We build alongside you, not just fund you.
Do I need quantum hardware access?
No. We start with simulation and hybrid approaches. We partner for hardware access when the use case requires it.
Do you incubate non-Quantum AI ventures?
Yes—when it compounds into Quantum or PQC roadmaps or solves frontier-class problems that align with our thesis.
Where are you based?
Indonesia-first, remote-capable, with a global network. We operate where the talent and opportunity are.
What equity stake do you take?
We typically co-found with minority equity (15-25%) based on the program and level of involvement. Terms are always founder-friendly and aligned with long-term success.
